Discover, apply for, and track agricultural government schemes designed to support farmers across the country. Your gateway to financial support, crop insurance, and agricultural guidance.

Platform Features

Scheme Discovery

Browse comprehensive government schemes tailored for farmers with detailed eligibility criteria and benefits. Find schemes that match your farming needs.

Easy Application

Submit applications online with guided forms and document upload capabilities for seamless processing. Track your application status in real-time.

Track Progress

Monitor your application status in real-time with notifications and updates throughout the approval process. Stay informed every step of the way.

Crop Information

Access detailed crop cultivation guides, seasonal recommendations, and location-based farming advice. Make informed farming decisions.

Location-Based

Mobile Friendly

About AgroSchemes

AgroSchemes is a comprehensive digital platform designed to bridge the gap between farmers and government agricultural schemes. Our mission is to make agricultural support programs accessible, transparent, and easy to navigate for farmers across the country.

With features like real-time application tracking, crop information database, and location-based recommendations, we empower farmers to make informed decisions and access the support they need to thrive in modern agriculture.

  • Simplified scheme discovery and application process
  • Real-time application status tracking
  • Comprehensive crop and farming information
  • Expert agricultural guidance and support
